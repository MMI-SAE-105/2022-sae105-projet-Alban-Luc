A human evolution

On the right track

We can see that on the road, there are less and less deaths, thanks mainly to prevention ads that can mark the most sensitive and therefore force them to be concentrated on the road, a mentality that will be transmitted from father / mother to son / daughter. In spite of this, in France, road violence is not part of our genes, for example, when a Frenchman goes to the United States, he is more likely to be stopped by the police or to be insulted by others for the way he drives. It's all a question of civic-mindedness.

One Earth

In 2022, more and more people will become aware of the ecological situation of our planet and therefore, will adopt an ecological behaviour and will start to sort their waste and avoid throwing it in the nature. We can also see the number of associations that are being created, whether for ecology such as "Initiatives Océanes" which cleans the beaches, for health such as "ASEF (Association Santé Environnement France)", for charity such as "Le secours populaire"... The government is putting in place a number of agro-ecological infrastructures in towns and villages, with the appearance of ecological corridors, which are regions of the territory that have the objective of connecting protected areas. We also see more and more dustbins to avoid pollution.
